关于我们

North America Chinese Culture Foundation

North America Chinese Culture Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to charitable, educational, and cultural purposes. The Foundation promotes cultural exchange, supports youth education, cares for seniors, encourages community service, and builds bridges of understanding between Chinese culture and North American communities.

北美中国文化基金会是一家致力于慈善、教育与文化公益事业的非营利组织。 基金会推动中华文化传承、中美文化交流、青少年教育支持、老人关怀、社区公益服务， 并促进不同族裔、不同文化之间的理解、尊重与合作。

Our Mission / 我们的使命

To preserve culture, support education, serve communities, and pass kindness from one generation to the next.

传承文化，支持教育，服务社区，让爱与善意在一代又一代之间延续。

Our Vision / 我们的愿景

To become a trusted cultural and charitable foundation connecting people, families, communities, and civilizations.

成为连接个人、家庭、社区与文明的可信赖文化公益基金会。

Core Values / 核心价值

Culture · Education · Compassion · Service · Responsibility

文化 · 教育 · 关爱 · 奉献 · 责任
