OUR MISSION

Culture, Education, Compassion, and Community

传承文化 · 支持教育 · 关爱生命 · 服务社区

North America Chinese Culture Foundation is dedicated to building a caring bridge between culture and community. Through education support, senior care, cultural heritage, animal rescue, emergency relief, and community service, we bring warmth, dignity, and hope to families across North America.

北美中华文化基金会致力于以文化为根、以教育为桥、以公益为心，推动敬老关爱、 助学圆梦、文化传承、动物救助、紧急援助与社区服务。

FEATURED STORY

Perhaps Your Donation Becomes Someone’s Light.

也许您的一次捐赠，就是别人一生的光。

Behind every program is a real person, a real family, and a real life waiting for hope. Your support may help a student continue school, bring dignity to a senior, rescue an animal, or help a family through an emergency.

每一个项目背后，都有一个真实的人、一个真实的家庭、一个正在等待希望的生命。 您的一份善意，可能让学生继续读书，让老人重拾尊严，让小动物重新获得生命，也让困难家庭渡过难关。
