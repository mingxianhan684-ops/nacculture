Our Mission / 我们的使命

Culture · Education · Charity · Community

North America Chinese Culture Foundation is organized exclusively for charitable, educational, and cultural purposes. We promote cultural exchange, support youth education, care for seniors, and serve families and communities in need.

北美中国文化基金会致力于慈善、教育与文化公益事业，推动中华文化传承、青少年教育支持、 老人关怀、社区公益服务，并促进北美多元文化之间的理解与交流。
