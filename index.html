Our Mission

Culture · Education · Compassion · Community

North America Chinese Culture Foundation is dedicated to cultural heritage, education support, senior care, animal rescue, emergency relief and community service.

北美中华文化基金会致力于文化传承、教育助学、敬老关怀、动物救助、紧急援助与社区公益服务。
