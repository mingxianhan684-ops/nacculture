Our Mission / 我们的使命

North America Chinese Culture Foundation is organized exclusively for charitable, educational, and cultural purposes. We support cultural exchange, youth education, senior care, and community service.

北美中国文化基金会致力于慈善、教育与文化公益事业，推动中美文化交流、 青少年教育、老人关怀与社区服务。

Our Programs / 公益项目
