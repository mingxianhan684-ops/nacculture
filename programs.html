Our Programs

基金会公益项目

🎓 Education Fund 教育助学基金

Support scholarships, books, school supplies, and educational opportunities for students.

帮助困难学生获得奖学金、学习用品、 图书和教育资源支持。

👴 Senior Care 老人关爱基金

Provide support, companionship and community services for senior citizens.

为老人提供关怀、探访、 节日慰问及社区服务。

🐶 Pet Rescue 宠物救助基金

Rescue homeless animals, provide medical care and adoption services.

救助流浪动物， 提供医疗救助及领养服务。

🏮 Cultural Exchange 文化交流项目

Promote Chinese culture and international cultural understanding.

推动中华文化传播， 促进国际文化交流。

👨‍🎓 Youth Leadership 青年领袖计划

Develop future leaders through camps, volunteer service and mentorship.

通过夏令营、志愿服务、 导师计划培养未来青年领袖。
